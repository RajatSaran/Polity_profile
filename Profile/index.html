Bishweswar Tudu

Union Minister of Jal Shakti and Tribal Affairs

About

Bishweswar Tudu is the Union Minister of Jal Shakti and Tribal Affairs in the Government of India. He is a member of the Bharatiya Janata Party (BJP) and has been representing the Sundargarh constituency in the Lok Sabha since 2014.

Tudu was born in 1967 in Sundargarh district, Odisha. He is a graduate in Economics and has a Master's degree in Public Administration. He started his political career as a student leader and was elected to the Sundargarh Zilla Parishad in 1995. He was elected to the Odisha Legislative Assembly in 2000 and 2004.

In 2014, Tudu was elected to the Lok Sabha from Sundargarh. He was appointed as the Minister of State for Tribal Affairs in the Narendra Modi government in 2014. He was promoted to the Cabinet rank in 2019 and given the additional charge of the Ministry of Jal Shakti.

Experience

  • Union Minister of Jal Shakti and Tribal Affairs (2019 - Present)
  • Minister of State for Tribal Affairs (2014 - 2019)
  • Member of the Odisha Legislative Assembly (2000 - 2004)
  • Member of the Sundargarh Zilla Parishad (1995 - 2000)

Projects

  • Sundargarh Irrigation Project
  • Sundargarh Tribal Development Project
